Calvin Information Center

We are a team of students working to solve a small but annoying problem that many Calvin Students face: the lack of information about their meal plan. We are working to design a kiosk and website that will allow students to check how many meals they have remaining easily and quickly, all without having to swipe for a meal.

Check back later for more details and information as we continue working on it.
